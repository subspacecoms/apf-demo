# Product Brief: The Outlandish Banana

## Executive Summary
[DRAFTING: A shopping experience that transforms the mundane commodity of a banana into a surrealist meme-driven event. Targeted at "regular" consumers looking for an absurd, memorable, and high-end delivery experience.]

## The Problem
[DRAFTING: Commodity fatigue. Bananas are treated as a cheap, disposable food item with no brand identity or emotional resonance.]

## The Solution
[DRAFTING: A surrealist e-commerce platform where the interface, branding, and packaging lean into the "outlandish." Selling bananas not as food, but as "objects of absurdity."]

## What Makes This Different
[DRAFTING: "Surrealist Meme" branding. The interface is not built for efficiency, but for immersion in a specific aesthetic. Packaging is over-the-top (e.g., velvet-lined boxes for single fruits).]

## Who This Serves
[DRAFTING: Regular consumers who enjoy internet culture, absurdity, or are looking for a unique gift/experience.]

## Success Criteria
[DRAFTING: Viral sharing of the unboxing experience; user engagement with the "outlandish" interface.]

## Scope
[DRAFTING: Web interface, packaging design, initial brand identity.]
