## Executive Summary
A stock screener designed to correlate global world events with financial sector performance. This tool aims to provide investors with insights into how geopolitical, environmental, and social events influence specific market segments, particularly focused on Clean Energy.

## The Problem
Investors often struggle to quantify the immediate and long-term impact of global news and events on specific industry sectors, leading to reactive rather than proactive investment strategies.

## The Solution
A platform that maps world events (e.g., climate accords, energy crises) to sector performance, allowing users to identify trends and correlations based on historical and real-time data.

## Scope
*   **In-Scope:** Correlation engine between events and sectors, focus on Clean Energy sector signals, basic event tracking.
*   **Out-of-Scope:** High-frequency trading execution, comprehensive asset management features.

## Vision
To become the primary intelligence layer for event-driven investing, expanding from Clean Energy to all major global sectors.
