# Clean Energy PRD

## 1. Executive Summary
A stock screener designed to correlate global world events with financial sector performance, specifically focused on Clean Energy.

## 2. Problem Statement
Investors struggle to quantify the impact of global news on industry sectors, leading to reactive strategies.

## 3. Vision
To be the primary intelligence layer for event-driven investing.

## 4. Target Audience
*   Individual Investors
*   Financial Analysts
*   Clean Energy Sector Specialists

## 5. Functional Requirements
### 5.1 Event Tracking
*   **FR-1.1**: The system shall track global world events (geopolitical, environmental, social).
*   **FR-1.2**: The system shall allow tagging events by type (e.g., Climate Accord, Energy Crisis).

### 5.2 Sector Correlation Engine
*   **FR-2.1**: The system shall correlate tracked events with Clean Energy sector stock performance.
*   **FR-2.2**: The system shall provide historical correlation data.
*   **FR-2.3**: The system shall provide real-time sector signals based on emerging events.

## 6. Non-Functional Requirements
*   **NFR-1**: Performance - Correlation calculations should update within 1 minute of a significant data point change.
*   **NFR-2**: Scalability - System should handle up to 10,000 concurrent users.

## 7. Scope & Constraints
*   **In-Scope**: Correlation engine, Clean Energy signals, basic event tracking.
*   **Out-of-Scope**: High-frequency trading execution, comprehensive asset management.
